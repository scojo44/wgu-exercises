    Lodging on Taniti

    Taniti features lodging options for a wide range of budgets. There's a world-class four-star resort, The Taniti Grand Hotel and Casino with all the amenities you would expect, several native family-owned hotels, and a variety of bed-and-breakfasts.

    • Tennis Courts
    • Exercise Room
    • Valet Parking
    • VIP Entrance
    • Conference Rooms
    • Slot machines, Roulette wheels, Blackjack, Video Poker, and more!
