    Visit Taniti!

    Taniti is a tropical island in the Pacific Ocean with almost 500 square miles of varied terrain to explore including both sandy and rocky beaches, a safe harbor, lush tropical rainforests, mountains, and even an active volcano. We are an indigenous population of about 20,000 people and we invite you to enjoy the island with us!
